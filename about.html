About Me

I am a cybersecurity enthusiast with a strong passion for securing digital systems, analyzing vulnerabilities, and implementing security solutions. Over the years, I have gained hands-on experience in penetration testing, network security, and ethical hacking.

My journey in cybersecurity started with a curiosity about how systems work and how they can be exploited. I have worked on various security projects, including vulnerability assessments, risk mitigation strategies, and security audits.

Beyond technical expertise, I am dedicated to spreading awareness about cybersecurity best practices. My goal is to help organizations and individuals strengthen their digital security and stay ahead of emerging cyber threats.
